What is MealFindr?

Since the start of the COVID19 pandemic, food insecurity in the state of Washington has nearly doubled! Local Seattle Eateries have created their own meal programs to help their community members. However, if you find yourself or your family facing food insecurity, you may not always know where to go for help. Thatâ€™s where MealFindr comes in! MealFindr was created to bridge that gap and provide users with a list of Seattle Eatery locations that offer a free meal to those who need it.

Our Mission

To provide a free resource to find restaurants and locations that offer no-cost meals to those facing food insecurity.
